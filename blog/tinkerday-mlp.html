Sharing Stories, Sparking Innovation – My Experience at TinkerDay Malappuram

TinkerDay Malappuram wasn't just another event—it was a celebration of innovation, curiosity, and fresh perspectives. Hosted at Zil Money HQ, Manjeri on October 26, 2024, the event gathered a vibrant crowd of thinkers, dreamers, and doers from across the region—and I had the privilege of taking the stage as one of the speakers.

The atmosphere was electric. From the moment I walked in, I was surrounded by exciting minds and powerful stories that reminded me why I do what I do at Neuraq Technologies. As a speaker, I had the chance to share my journey, the challenges we've faced building tech solutions, and how innovation can be sparked in the smallest of ideas.

The event was filled with interactive sessions, open conversations, and moments that truly inspired. Whether it was engaging with students, networking with fellow speakers, or just listening to the amazing ideas being shared—it was a day that left a lasting impact.

What made TinkerDay special was its focus on grassroots innovation. Unlike larger tech conferences, this event had an intimate, community-driven feel where local talent could shine and connect with like-minded individuals.

TinkerDay proved that Malappuram is brimming with talent and passion—and I'm excited to see where these new connections and conversations lead. Events like these are crucial for fostering local tech ecosystems and inspiring the next generation of innovators.
